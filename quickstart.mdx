<Tabs>
	<Tab title="Web & Desktop">
		### Install the browser extension

		First things first — <Icon icon="puzzle-piece" size={16} /> [install the browser extension](/install-extension).
        It's the fastest way to save anything you find online.
        Once installed, <Icon icon="thumbtack" size={16} /> pin it to your toolbar so it's always one click away.

		### Save your first bookmark

		Try it out — click <Icon icon="cloud" size={16} /> icon on any page to save it. That's it, your first bookmark is in Raindrop.io.

		<Frame style={{padding: 0}}>![](/assets/extension-button.png)</Frame>

		<Info>There are many other ways to [add bookmarks](/bookmarks) (including on mobile), you can even [upload your own files](/files).</Info>

		### Organize

		When saving you can pick a [collection](/collections) and add [tags](/tags). Think of collections as folders, and tags as labels like `to-read`, `important`, or `gift`.
		
		<Frame style={{padding: 0}}>![](/assets/extension-intro.png)</Frame>

		<Tip>**Pro tip**: want to come back to something later? [Set a reminder](/reminders) — we'll notify you when it's time.</Tip>

		### Highlight what matters

        You've saved a page, but what about the key parts? Select any text, right-click and choose **Save highlight** — next time you open that bookmark, your highlights are right there, across all your devices.

		<Frame style={{padding: 0}}>![](/assets/highlights-intro.png)</Frame>

		### Find what you've saved

		Access your bookmarks and highlights from anywhere — pick the [web app](https://app.raindrop.io), [desktop app](https://raindrop.io/download), or [mobile app](/mobile-app), whichever suits you best. Everything stays in sync automatically.

		![](/assets/devices.png)
	</Tab>

	<Tab title="Mobile app">
		### Install the app

		Download Raindrop.io for <Icon icon="apple" size={16} /> [iOS](https://raindrop.io/r/app/ios) or <Icon icon="android" iconType="duotone" size={16} /> [Android](https://raindrop.io/r/app/android) and sign in to your account.

        <Frame style={{padding: 0}}>![](/assets/mobile-app-intro.png)</Frame>

		### Save your first bookmark

		Open any page in your browser or app, tap <Icon icon="arrow-up-from-square" size={16} /> **Share**, and choose <Icon icon="cloud" iconType="duotone" size={16} /> **Raindrop.io** from the list. That's it — your first bookmark is saved.

		<Check>
            On Android it works right away.   
            On iOS you may need to [enable the share extension](/mobile-app) first.
        </Check>

		<Frame style={{padding: '20px 0 0 0'}}><img src="/assets/ios-share-sheet.png" style={{maxHeight: '333px'}} /></Frame>

		### Organize

		When saving you can pick a [collection](/collections) and add [tags](/tags). Think of collections as folders, and tags as labels like `to-read`, `important`, or `gift`.

		<Tip>**Pro tip**: want to come back to something later? [Set a reminder](/reminders) — we'll notify you when it's time.</Tip>

		<Frame style={{padding: '20px 0 0 0'}}><img src="/assets/ios-edit-bookmark.png" style={{maxHeight: '333px'}} /></Frame>

        ### Highlight what matters

		Open any saved page in the app and select text to save it as a [highlight](/highlights). Your highlights sync across all your devices automatically.

		<Frame style={{padding: '20px 0 0 0'}}><img src="/assets/ios-highlight.png" style={{maxHeight: '333px'}} /></Frame>

		### Find what you've saved

		Head to the Raindrop.io app — everything you've saved through Share is here. Your library also syncs with the [web](https://app.raindrop.io) and [desktop](https://raindrop.io/download) apps automatically.

        ![](/assets/devices.png)
	</Tab>
</Tabs>

### Chat with your library

Over time your library becomes your personal knowledge base. [Stella](/stella) lets you chat with it — ask questions, get summaries, find things you vaguely remember, or have it organize your collections for you.

<Frame style={{padding:0}}>![Stella](/assets/stella-intro.png)</Frame>
