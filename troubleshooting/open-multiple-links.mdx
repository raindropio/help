title: "Can't open multiple links at once"
icon: "window-restore"
---

You click **Open all bookmarks** or select several bookmarks and click **Open**, but nothing happens — or only one tab opens. This is because your browser blocks pop-ups from Raindrop.io by default.

<Tabs sync={false}>
	<Tab title="Chrome / Edge / Brave">
		After you try to open multiple tabs, a small icon appears in the address bar. Click it and allow pop-ups for Raindrop.io.

		{/* Screenshot: Chrome pop-up blocked icon */}

		**Alternative:** paste `chrome://settings/content/popups` into the address bar, scroll to **Allow**, click **Add**, and enter `[*.]raindrop.io`.
	</Tab>

	<Tab title="Safari">
		<Steps>
			<Step>
				Right-click the address bar and choose **Settings for app.raindrop.io**
			</Step>
			<Step>
				Set **Pop-up Windows** to **Allow**
			</Step>
		</Steps>
	</Tab>

	<Tab title="Firefox">
		After you try to open multiple tabs, Firefox shows a notification bar at the top. Click **Preferences** and select **Allow pop-ups for app.raindrop.io**.

		{/* Screenshot: Firefox pop-up blocked bar */}

		**Alternative:** go to `about:preferences#privacy`, scroll to **Permissions**, click **Exceptions** next to **Block pop-up windows**, add `https://app.raindrop.io`, and click **Allow**.
	</Tab>
</Tabs>

## Limitations

Only the first 40 bookmarks in a collection will be opened at once. If you need to open more, scroll down the collection to load additional bookmarks before clicking **Open all**.
