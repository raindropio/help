---
title: "Extension keeps asking for permissions"
---

This only happens in Safari. The Raindrop.io extension works without any special permissions by default, but two features need access to web page content:

- [Highlights](/highlights) — to display saved highlights when you revisit a page
- [Saved page indicator](/install-extension#enabling-saved-page-indicator) — to check if the current page is already bookmarked

When you enable either of these, Safari asks for permission. If the permission flow isn't completed fully — for example, you click "Allow for One Day" instead of "Always Allow" — Safari will keep asking every time.

## How to fix

<Steps>
	<Step>
		Click the Raindrop.io icon, then your profile picture, and go to **Settings**
	</Step>
	<Step>
		In the **Permissions** section, uncheck and then recheck **Highlights**
	</Step>
	<Step>
		Choose **Always Allow** or **Always Allow on Every Website** when prompted
	</Step>
	<Step>
		Restart Safari
	</Step>
	<Step>
		Open Safari **Settings → Websites**, find **Raindrop.io**, and set it to **Allow** for other websites
	</Step>
</Steps>
