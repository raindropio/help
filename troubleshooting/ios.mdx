## Share extension crashes or doesn't appear

A full reinstall usually fixes this:

<Steps>
	<Step>
		Go to **Settings** → **General** → **iPhone Storage** → **Raindrop.io**
	</Step>
	<Step>
		Tap **Delete App** — this removes the app and all cached data
	</Step>
	<Step>
		Install Raindrop.io again from the App Store
	</Step>
</Steps>

## Share extension doesn't appear in the share sheet

If you don't see Raindrop.io in the share menu at all:

- Make sure the [Raindrop.io app](/mobile-app) is installed — the share extension is part of the app
- Restart your device — iOS sometimes doesn't register new share extensions until a reboot
- If it still doesn't show up, follow the [setup steps](/mobile-app#enabling-share-button) to enable it manually
