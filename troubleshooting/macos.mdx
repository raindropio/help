## Resetting the app cache

If the macOS app is showing stale data or not working correctly, clearing the cache often helps:

<Steps>
	<Step>
		Open Finder and click **Go** → **Go to Folder**
	</Step>
	<Step>
		Paste `~/Library/Application Support/Raindrop.io` and press Enter
	</Step>
	<Step>
		Delete everything inside this folder
	</Step>
	<Step>
		Reopen the Raindrop.io app
	</Step>
</Steps>
