[Notion](https://www.notion.so/) is an all-in-one workspace for notes, docs, and databases. With this integration you can sync bookmarks and highlights to Notion pages via [IFTTT](/integrations/ifttt).

## Available applets

<CardGroup cols={1}>
	<Card title="Save new Raindrop.io bookmarks to Notion" icon="/assets/icon-ifttt.png" href="https://ifttt.com/applets/Hea7nhpG-save-new-raindrop-io-bookmarks-to-notion" horizontal>
		Each new bookmark becomes a Notion page automatically
	</Card>

	<Card title="Create a Notion page for each new Raindrop.io highlight" icon="/assets/icon-ifttt.png" href="https://ifttt.com/applets/n9WkF86C-create-a-notion-page-for-each-new-raindrop-io-highlight" horizontal>
		Highlights are captured with their text, color, and source URL
	</Card>
</CardGroup>
