[YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/) is a video sharing platform by Google. With this integration you can automatically save liked videos and new subscriptions as bookmarks via [IFTTT](/integrations/ifttt).

## Available applets

<CardGroup cols={1}>
	<Card title="Save liked YouTube videos to Raindrop.io bookmarks" icon="/assets/icon-ifttt.png" href="https://ifttt.com/applets/DJyFrvNd-save-liked-youtube-videos-to-raindrop-io-bookmarks" horizontal>
		Every liked video becomes a bookmark you can revisit, tag, and organize
	</Card>

	<Card title="Save new YouTube subscriptions to Raindrop.io bookmarks" icon="/assets/icon-ifttt.png" href="https://ifttt.com/applets/EuqeXwiR-save-new-youtube-subscriptions-to-raindrop-io-bookmarks" horizontal>
		Each new subscription is saved as a bookmark you can revisit, tag, and organize
	</Card>
</CardGroup>
