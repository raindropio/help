[Google Drive](https://drive.google.com/) is a cloud storage service. With this integration your bookmarks are automatically backed up to your Google Drive account once a day.

<Note>Automatic backups require a [Pro](/premium-features) plan.</Note>

## Setting up

<Steps>
	<Step>
		Go to [Settings → Backups](https://app.raindrop.io/settings/backups)
	</Step>
	<Step>
		Connect your Google Drive account
	</Step>
</Steps>

Once connected, a backup is created once a day when there are changes.
