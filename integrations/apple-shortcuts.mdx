[Apple Shortcuts](https://support.apple.com/guide/shortcuts/welcome/ios) is a built-in automation app for iOS, iPadOS, and macOS. With this integration you can save and organize bookmarks by voice with Siri, from the home screen, or as part of a multi-step workflow.

## Setting up

<Steps>
    <Step>
        Open the **Shortcuts** app
    </Step>
    <Step>
        Tap **+** to create a new shortcut
    </Step>
    <Step>
        Search for **Raindrop.io** in the actions list
    </Step>
</Steps>

## Available actions

| Action | What it does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Save bookmark** | Save a URL to a specific collection |
| **Search bookmarks** | Find bookmarks by keyword |
| **Get bookmark** | Retrieve details of a specific bookmark |

## Example shortcuts

- **Save clipboard link** — save whatever URL is in your clipboard to a chosen collection
- **Save current Safari page** — one-tap shortcut from the home screen or widget
- **Daily reading list** — automatically collect links from a specific source into a collection
