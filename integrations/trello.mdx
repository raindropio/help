[Trello](https://trello.com/) is a visual project management tool with boards, lists, and cards. With this integration you can automatically turn bookmarks into Trello cards via [IFTTT](/integrations/ifttt).

## Available applets

<CardGroup cols={1}>
	<Card title="New bookmarks → cards" icon="/assets/icon-ifttt.png" href="https://ifttt.com/applets/qnbTux7a-create-a-trello-card-for-each-new-raindrop-io-item" horizontal>
		Each saved link becomes a card on your chosen board — useful for tracking resources or planning projects
	</Card>
</CardGroup>

Browse [ready-made applets](https://ifttt.com/connect/raindrop/trello) or [create your own](https://ifttt.com/create) — including multi-step workflows. Learn how such [integrations work](/integrations/ifttt)
