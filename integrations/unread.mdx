[Unread](https://www.goldenhillsoftware.com/unread/) is an RSS reader with beautiful typography for iPhone and iPad. With this integration you can save articles directly to your bookmarks from the article actions menu.

## Setting up

Enable `Raindrop.io` in **Settings → Article Actions**.
