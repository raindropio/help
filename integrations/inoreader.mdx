[Inoreader](https://www.inoreader.com/) is an RSS feed reader and content hub. With this integration you can save articles with a single click or automatically sync your starred items.

## Adding a share button

Inoreader has [built-in Raindrop.io integration](https://www.inoreader.com/blog/2025/05/raindrop-io-integration-now-available-in-inoreader.html). Enable it in your Inoreader sharing settings.

## Syncing starred items automatically

You can automatically save your starred Inoreader articles using IFTTT.
