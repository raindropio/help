[Feedbin](https://feedbin.com/) is a fast, simple RSS feed reader. With this integration you can save articles to your bookmarks with one click from the sharing menu.

<Steps>
	<Step>
		Go to Feedbin [Share settings](https://feedbin.com/settings/sharing)

		<Frame style={{padding: 0}}>![](/assets/feedbin-1.png)</Frame>
	</Step>
	<Step>
		Enable **Raindrop.io**
	</Step>
	<Step>
		Now when you click share button you can choose Raindrop.io as destination
		
		<Frame style={{padding: 0}}>![](/assets/feedbin-2.png)</Frame>
	</Step>
</Steps>
