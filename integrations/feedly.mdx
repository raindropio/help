[Feedly](https://feedly.com/) is an RSS feed reader and news aggregator. With this integration you can save articles with a single click or automatically sync your Read Later list.

## Adding a share button

<Steps>
	<Step>
		Go to Feedly [Saving & Sharing settings](https://feedly.com/i/account/saving-sharing)
	</Step>
	<Step>
		Add a custom sharing tool with this URL:
		```
		https://app.raindrop.io/add?link=${url}&title=${title}
		```
	</Step>
</Steps>
