[Readwise](https://readwise.io) is a tool for revisiting and learning from your highlights. With this integration you can import your Readwise Reader library and sync highlights from your bookmarks directly to Readwise.

## Importing from Readwise Reader

You can transfer your bookmarks and highlights from Readwise Reader to Raindrop.io.

<Steps>
	<Step>
		[Export CSV file](https://read.readwise.io/profile) from Readwise Reader
	</Step>
	<Step>
		Go to [Settings → Import](https://app.raindrop.io/settings/import)
	</Step>
	<Step>
		Upload the file
	</Step>
</Steps>

Learn more about [importing bookmarks from other apps](/import).
