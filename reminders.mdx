You save an article to read later, a link to check before a deadline, or a page to revisit after an event — and then forget about it. Reminders bring bookmarks back to your attention at the right time via **push notification** or **email**.

<Note>
	Reminders are a [premium feature](/premium-features).
</Note>

## Setting a reminder

<Tabs borderBottom>
	<Tab title="Web & Desktop">
		<Steps>
			<Step>
				Right-click a bookmark to open the context menu and select **Edit**.

                <Frame style={{padding:0}}>![Web archive of a saved page](/assets/bookmark-context-menu.png)</Frame>
			</Step>
			<Step>
				Click the **Reminder** bell icon and pick a date and time

				<Frame style={{padding:0}}><img src="/assets/bookmark-edit.png" /></Frame>
			</Step>
		</Steps>
	</Tab>

	<Tab title="Browser extension">
		<Steps>
			<Step>
				Click the Raindrop.io extension icon.

                <Frame style={{padding: 0}}>![](/assets/extension-button.png)</Frame>
			</Step>
			<Step>
				Click the **Reminder** bell icon and pick a date and time

                <Frame style={{padding: 0}}>![](/assets/extension-intro.png)</Frame>
			</Step>
			<Step>
				Click **Save** for a new bookmark, or just close the extension window
			</Step>
		</Steps>
	</Tab>

	<Tab title="iOS">
		<Steps>
			<Step>
				Long-press a bookmark and choose **Edit**.

				<Frame style={{padding: '20px 0 0 0'}}><img src="/assets/ios-edit-bookmark.png" style={{maxHeight: '333px'}} /></Frame>
			</Step>
			<Step>
				Tap **Add reminder** and pick a date and time

				<Frame style={{padding: '20px 0 0 0'}}><img src="/assets/ios-add-reminder.png" style={{maxHeight: '333px'}} /></Frame>
			</Step>
		</Steps>
	</Tab>

	<Tab title="Android">
		<Steps>
			<Step>
				Tap the <Icon icon="ellipsis" size={16} /> icon next to a bookmark to open the edit screen.

				<Frame style={{padding: '20px 0 0 0'}}><img src="/assets/android-edit-bookmark.png" style={{maxHeight: '333px'}} /></Frame>
			</Step>
			<Step>
				Tap **Reminder** and pick a date and time

				<Frame style={{padding: '20px 0 0 0'}}><img src="/assets/android-reminder.png" style={{maxHeight: '333px'}} /></Frame>
			</Step>
		</Steps>
	</Tab>
</Tabs>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
	<Accordion title="How will I be notified?">
		You'll receive a push notification (if the mobile app is installed) or an email at the scheduled time.
	</Accordion>

	<Accordion title="What timezone is used?">
		Your local timezone. The reminder fires at the date and time you see in the picker.
	</Accordion>

	<Accordion title="Can I set recurring reminders?">
		Not yet. Each reminder is a one-time notification.
	</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
